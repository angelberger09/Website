# 2026-06-28 — Portfolio archive ticket receipt route

## Summary

Refined the Portfolio future archive shelf so the three future archive ticket cards read as a bound receipt route instead of a loose ticket row.

## Interface change

- Updated `app/portfolio-feed-receipt-seal-pass.css`.
- Strengthened `.portfolio-archive-ticket-board` as a visible three-part receipt deck with a clipped `three part receipt route` cue.
- Added tape strips, calmer shadows, and responsive/reduced-motion safeguards to the ticket cards.

## Why

The Portfolio future shelf had public-safe field cards, rule slips, and receipt/seal cues, but the ticket path between the heading and ledger could still scan as a neighboring row rather than part of one public archive handoff. This keeps the no-fake-project boundary intact while making the path from `Name the public piece` to `Add an honest preview` to `Point to the next shelf` more visibly bound.
