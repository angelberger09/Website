# Notes support photo slips

Timestamp: 2026-06-25 16:34 ET

## Changed

- Added `app/notes-support-photo-slips-pass.css` so Notes support and reader-state notes include abstract paper/photo centers with clipped label slips.
- Imported the pass from `app/layout.jsx` after the Notes path photo board pass.
- Updated pages, components, and quality metrics matrices to record that the Notes support/state surfaces are no longer text-only paper cards.

## Why

The Notes index already had a paper reader board, published-note visuals, a gallery, and path photo cards, but its support and reader-state notes still had text-only faces. Current active lessons support honest abstract visual centers where they clarify structure without inventing note art, fake screenshots, or changing the Blog feed contract.
