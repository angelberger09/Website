## Goal

Make the Notes contact-sheet gallery read as one calm reading-room docket, not a detached intro followed by a separate horizontal card row.

## Boundary

Use only existing published-note data, existing route links, and paper/collage styling. Do not invent note artwork, screenshots, private writing material, fake posts, or new content claims.

## Visible proof

- The Notes gallery board has a visible `contact sheet path` label.
- The section intro is a cut-paper docket with a `published notes` slip.
- The intro body copy sits on a readable paper snippet.
- The horizontal gallery has a dashed shelf thread and stronger lifted-paper depth.
- Mobile fallback preserves readability and removes card rotation.

## Satisfaction

Partially satisfied. This pass strengthens the Notes gallery section, but deployed visual review should still confirm that the entire Notes index reads as one calm room rather than many accumulated surface passes.
