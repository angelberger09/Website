# PageIntro Photo Stamp Ledger

Timestamp: 2026-06-29 00:21 ET

## Change

Added a shared PageIntro photo-stamp ledger pass and loaded it from the root layout.

## Why

The active Website direction says major pages should behave like calm rooms with composed hero boards, content-bearing photo centers, paper snippets, and route continuity. The shared PageIntro photo card and room key were already present, but the relationship between the photo card, room key, and route shelf was only partially bound. This refinement adds small stamped paper cues, tape fragments, and stronger lifted-paper shadows without changing public copy or inventing content.
