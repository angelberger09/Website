## Purpose

Make the shared non-home route canvas read as one calm paper room instead of leaving individual route dockets, shelves, and snippets floating independently on the global background.

## Boundary

- Do not add unverified page content, product material, post material, portfolio claims, or private material.
- Do not change routing, accessible labels, or public page copy.
- Keep the effect asset-backed and CSS-only using existing grain/scratch variables.
- Keep mobile fallbacks readable and avoid heavy animation.

## Visible check

On shared route pages, the reader should now see a large clipped paper backing sheet behind the route content, a subtle left `room path` binding strip, a small `public room` tag near the start of the layout, and existing page-specific cards still above the backing and readable.

## Follow-up validation

Deployed visual review should confirm that the shared backing increases room cohesion without making route content feel boxed-in or adding too much repeated label noise.
