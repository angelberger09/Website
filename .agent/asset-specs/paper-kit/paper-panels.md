# Paper Panel Asset Specs

These assets replace generic bordered sections with real torn paper fields and colored backing papers.

## Shared requirements

- Folder: `public/paper/cards/`
- Final format: transparent PNG
- Preview matte: black background
- Edge style: torn, fibery, alpha-clipped
- Surface: visible paper fiber, subtle wrinkle, no hard digital rectangle
- Shadow: preferably separate or very soft if included

## Large cream panels

### `paper-panel-large-cream-01.png`

Purpose: main hero paper field or large section background.

Aspect ratio: 16:9 or 3:2.  
Recommended size: 2200 × 1400 px.  
Colors: warm cream `#fff7e8`, fiber beige `#e6d6b8`, faint gray grain.  
Shape: large torn rectangle, gently warped, irregular edges on all sides.

Masking: transparent outside the panel. Preserve natural edge feathering.

Use: behind hero text or large intro section.

Acceptance check: big enough to carry text; not so textured that type becomes hard to read.

### `paper-panel-large-cream-02.png`

Purpose: alternate large cream panel with more vertical paper grain.

Aspect ratio: 16:9 or 3:2.  
Recommended size: 2200 × 1400 px.  
Colors: same cream family but slightly cooler.  
Shape: straighter left/right edges, rougher top/bottom edges.

Use: alternate hero/section background.

### `paper-panel-large-cream-03.png`

Purpose: cleaner large cream panel for calmer layouts.

Aspect ratio: 16:9 or 3:2.  
Recommended size: 2200 × 1400 px.  
Colors: pale cream, very low contrast texture.  
Shape: subtle torn edges, less wobble.

Use: pages with more content density.
