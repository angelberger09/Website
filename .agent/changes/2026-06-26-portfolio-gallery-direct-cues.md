# Portfolio gallery direct cues

Timestamp: 2026-06-26 09:30 ET

## Changed

- Added `app/portfolio-gallery-direct-cues-pass.css`.
- Loaded the pass from `app/layout.jsx` after the base Portfolio gallery pass.
- Replaced the repeated Portfolio gallery corner-chip pseudo labels with direct, source-backed paper cues: `Public piece`, `Archive note`, `Systems lane`, `Image rule`, and `Archive path`.

## Why

The Portfolio gallery already had an alternating paper/photo shelf, but its corner chips repeated broad generated labels. Current direct-label, paper-snippet, gallery, and photo-led surface guidance supports making each visible gallery chip name the role of that specific card without inventing screenshots, fake project images, or private case-study material.
