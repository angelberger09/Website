## Goal

Make the Portfolio future archive shelf read as one composed public-card shelf instead of a set of adjacent cards, while preserving the honest future-feed boundary and avoiding fake project assets.

## Boundaries

- Do not invent Portfolio work, screenshots, client records, private case studies, or future routes.
- Do not hide the public-facing feed contract or readiness copy.
- Do not create noisy material effects that reduce legibility.
- Keep mobile and reduced-motion behavior calm.
