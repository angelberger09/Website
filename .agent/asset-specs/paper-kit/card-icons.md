# Card Icon Illustration Asset Specs

These assets are small composed paper/collage illustrations used inside feature cards.

## Shared requirements

- Folder: `public/paper/cards/`
- Final format: transparent PNG
- Preview matte: black background for edge inspection
- Style: small paper collage object, soft handmade, not too detailed
- Use: visual identity for cards and future internal page headers

## `icon-notes-notepad-leaf-01.png`

Purpose: decorative icon for Notes.

Aspect ratio: 1:1.  
Recommended size: 900 × 900 px.  
Content: small spiral/lined notepad with a sage leaf sprig taped or resting on it.

Colors: cream paper, sage leaf, warm gray linework, tiny brass/cream detail.

Masking: transparent outside composed object.

## `icon-sample-paper-swatches-01.png`

Purpose: decorative icon for Sample.

Aspect ratio: 1:1.  
Recommended size: 900 × 900 px.  
Content: small stack of paper swatches in cream, lavender, sage, and peach, slightly offset.

## `icon-source-botanical-clip-01.png`

Purpose: decorative icon for Source.

Content: small note or tag paper clipped with a botanical pressed stem.

## `icon-home-paper-stack-01.png`

## `icon-blog-journal-01.png`

Purpose: future blog/journal icon.

Recommended size: 900 × 900 px.

## `icon-portfolio-polaroid-01.png`

Purpose: future portfolio/gallery icon.

Aspect ratio: 1:1.  
Recommended size: 900 × 900 px.  
Content: small overlapping polaroid frames with transparent or dark photo windows.

## Acceptance checklist

- transparent around complete icon
- readable at small card size
- decorative but not visually loud
- matches paper kit colors and textures
- no black matte in final export
