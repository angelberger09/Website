## Mismatch

## Goal

Make the closing homepage path feel like one calm room-exit docket: paper-first, physically lifted, connected to the shared continuity thread, and easy to scan without adding fake content or new navigation.

## Visible proof

- The closing note gains stronger lifted-paper shadow and backing depth.
- A clipped `exit docket` label appears behind the closing surface.
- The abstract `next room` face now names the direct About/Notes path.
- A dashed thread visually connects the note to the shared continuity trail.
- The tag and heading gain smaller clipped paper slips, keeping supporting text in paper snippets.
