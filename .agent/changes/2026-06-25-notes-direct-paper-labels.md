# Notes Direct Paper Labels

Timestamp: 2026-06-25 20:09 ET

## Changed

- Added `app/notes-direct-labels-pass.css`.
- Loaded the pass from `app/layout.jsx` after `app/notes-gallery-pass.css`.
- Replaced visible decorative numeric stamps on Notes gallery cards with human-facing paper labels such as `latest` and `note shelf`.
- Replaced visible numeric support-card pins with direct paper labels such as `safe shelf`, `reader`, and `return`.

## Why

The active direct-label and paper-card lessons are still partially satisfied across secondary note surfaces. The Notes gallery and support cards already use paper/photo structure, but their visible numeric pins made the route feel more indexed than studio-like. This pass keeps the same honest published-note and support content while making those card cues warmer, more direct, and less decorative.

## Satisfaction state

Partially satisfied: the Notes room now has direct paper labels on these specific gallery/support surfaces; broader deployed visual review is still needed for remaining route surfaces.
