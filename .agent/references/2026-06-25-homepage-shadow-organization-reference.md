## Public-safe reference summary

The referenced homepage screenshot shows the live `/Website/` route with the shared header, first-screen route contact sheet, and the beginning of the `Front room` section. The visual feedback is about two reusable homepage concerns:

1. Paper and card surfaces need stronger visible drop shadows so they read as lifted layers.
