# Homepage reading folio refinement

Date: 2026-06-27 09:50 ET

## Interface change

Updated `app/home-first-read-compass-pass.css` so the homepage quick reading path reads more like one ordered paper folio instead of a loose horizontal strip of small slips.

Visible changes:

- adds a clipped vertical `page order` rail on the left side of the homepage reading map;
- adds a `follow gently` sticky paper cue at the end of the chapter trail;
- adds a dashed left binding line to the chapter trail;
- strengthens clipped slip silhouettes, shadows, and alternating placement;
- keeps mobile fallbacks readable and removes rotations on narrow screens.

## Why

The active `.agent` state says the homepage should be organized as a human-readable left-docked editorial path, and every major page should read as a calm room with a guided sequence. The existing homepage reading map was already compact and useful, but it could still read as a row of separate small cards rather than one bound path through the front page.

## Satisfaction state

Partially satisfied → refined.

## Boundaries preserved

- No route, copy, data, product, portfolio, note, or private-content changes.
- No fake imagery, fake products, fake posts, fake portfolio work, or fake shop links.
- Anchor links and focus visibility remain intact.
- Uses existing CSS/material language only.
