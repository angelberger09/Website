# Crinkled paper material feedback

Timestamp: 2026-06-24 22:58 ET

Status: active feedback

Source: Screenshot review of `angelberger09/Website` Store page material treatment.

## Public-safe summary

Paper surfaces should feel more physically handled and lightly crinkled instead of flat, smooth, and pristine.

## Extracted signal

The material system needs subtle crinkle cues: soft crease shadows, uneven tonal pressure, slight fold-like texture, or paper handling marks. The effect should stay cozy and readable, not dirty or harsh.

## Target state

- Paper surfaces feel gently handled.
- Large paper areas show subtle crinkle or crease variation.
- The crinkle feeling supports handmade collage.
- Text remains easy to read.

- COMP-005 Shared Site Chrome

## Implementation boundary

Apply as material styling, texture overlays, or shared CSS treatment. Do not use harsh realism or reduce readability.
