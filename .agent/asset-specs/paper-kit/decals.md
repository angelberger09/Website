# Paper Decal and Backing Scrap Asset Specs

These assets provide color and layering behind cream cards without relying on hard UI blocks.

## Shared requirements

- Folder: `public/paper/decals/`
- Final format: transparent PNG
- Preview matte: black background
- Style: small torn paper swatches, alpha-clipped edges, subtle paper grain
- Use: behind cards, notes, polaroids, section titles, and icon clusters

## `torn-paper-sage-small-01.png`

## Acceptance checklist

- transparent outside paper shapes
- black matte fully removed
- colors match brand palette and stay soft
- scraps layer naturally without feeling like geometric UI blobs
