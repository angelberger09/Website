# Public Safety Filter

The `.agent/` folder is public. Treat every file as something a visitor, collaborator, or search engine could read.

## Safe to store

- design principles
- copy tone rules
- public repo structure
- public URLs
- distilled workflow lessons
- semantic item relationships
- decisions and tradeoffs
- quality metrics

## Do not store

- raw conversation transcripts
- private personal details
- private drafts or vulnerable writing
- medical, financial, legal, or family details
- sensitive context that is not needed for the project lesson
- direct quotes from private feedback if the distilled rule is enough

## Preferred transformation

Instead of storing:

> Angel said the page feels wrong because of a private context.

Store:

> Lesson: The homepage should feel authored, calm, and human-facing before technical details appear.

## Public-safe checklist

Before committing a feedback lesson, confirm:

- The text is about the website, not private life.
- The lesson can stand alone without conversation context.
- The wording is respectful and neutral.
- The lesson helps future work behave better.
- No private draft text is included.
