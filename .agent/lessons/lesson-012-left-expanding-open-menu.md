# LESSON-012 — Make the Pages menu open left and stay visually unboxed

Date: 2026-06-23 23:40 ET  
Status: active

## Context

A screenshot review showed the Pages menu opening downward as a large rounded panel with its own border and background.

## Feedback or signal

The Pages menu should expand left from the Pages control, be open by default, and avoid its own border or panel background.

## Interpretation

The navigation should feel like part of the top header surface instead of a separate dropdown card. The menu should reveal laterally across the header line, not vertically down the page.

## Rule created

Treat the Pages menu as an always-visible or default-open horizontal navigation reveal that expands left from the Pages button. Remove the separate dropdown panel look unless mobile layout requires a fallback.

## Applies to

- COMP-002 Folder Pages Menu
- DESIGN-012 Unboxed Left-Expanding Menu
- INT-009 Leftward Default-Open Menu Reveal

## Metric

METRIC-011: The Pages links should be visible/open by default, expand left from the Pages control, and remain readable without a distinct dropdown border or panel background.
