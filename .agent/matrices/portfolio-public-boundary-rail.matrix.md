## Purpose

Make the Portfolio `Future archive shelf` public-boundary note easier to scan as a paper-room promise before visitors reach the future archive card requirements.

## Source state

The Portfolio future archive shelf already used:

- `portfolio-feed-board--shelf`
- `portfolio-feed-shelf-intro`
- `portfolio-archive-ticket-board`
- `portfolio-feed-drawer-path-pass.css`

The copy was public-safe and accurate, but the boundary note still read more like one paper card in a larger shelf than a clear transition from safety promise to archive card path.

## Target state

The section should:

- preserve the existing public-safe Portfolio copy and future feed contract;
- avoid fake screenshots, invented case studies, or private project details;
- turn the boundary promise into a more legible paper ledger;
- give the visitor a quick right-side cue for safe publishing, honest previews, and quiet source handling;
- introduce the archive-ticket path with a small paper slip instead of relying only on section spacing;
- stay readable on mobile and avoid motion-heavy effects.
