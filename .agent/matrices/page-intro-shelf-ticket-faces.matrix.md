## Quality checks

- The shelf label should read as a paper ticket-board label, not a generic UI chip.
- Each shelf item should have a visible image-like face, route cue, and readable copy side.
- Texture and shadow should be visible at screenshot scale without reducing contrast.
- Mobile should keep a calm one-column reading path.
- The visuals must remain abstract and route-specific; they must not imply fake products, unavailable portfolio screenshots, private notes, or completed shop inventory.
