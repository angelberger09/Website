## Purpose

Make the existing Notes `After the shelf` continuity cards read as one guided reader path instead of three independent card tiles.

## Boundaries

- Do not change Blog data, feed URLs, public note slugs, or route links.
- Do not invent note screenshots, private writing material, fake posts, or fake imagery.
- Preserve native links, focus states, and mobile stacking.
- Use CSS-only paper structure on the existing public-safe markup.
