# Fastener Asset Specs

These assets sell the physical collage feeling: tape, clips, rings, tacks, and pins.

## Shared requirements

- Folder: `public/paper/fasteners/`
- Final format: transparent PNG
- Preview matte: black background
- Style: soft photographed/illustrated paper-craft object, not hyperreal stock imagery
- Use: decorative anchors on paper cards, note stacks, polaroids, and headers

## `tape-masking-horizontal-01.png`

Purpose: horizontal masking tape strip.

Aspect ratio: 5:1.  
Recommended size: 900 × 180 px.  
Colors: translucent beige tape, warm cream fibers, slightly darker torn ends.

Masking: transparent outside tape; tape itself should be semi-translucent.

## `tape-masking-diagonal-01.png`

Purpose: diagonal masking tape strip.

Aspect ratio: 5:1 canvas with tape rotated.  
Recommended size: 900 × 300 px.  
Content: same tape material, angled within transparent canvas.

Use: corners of notes/polaroids.

## `tape-masking-short-01.png`

Purpose: short tape tab.

Aspect ratio: 3:1.  
Recommended size: 600 × 200 px.  
Use: small note/card attachments.

## `tape-cream-soft-01.png`

Purpose: softer cream tape with low contrast.

Aspect ratio: 5:1.  
Recommended size: 900 × 180 px.  
Colors: pale cream, subtle fiber, very soft edge.

## `paperclip-silver-01.png`

Purpose: silver paperclip.

Aspect ratio: 1:2.  
Recommended size: 500 × 900 px.  
Colors: warm silver/gray, gentle highlight, low contrast shadow.

Masking: transparent inside and outside clip loops.

## `paperclip-gold-01.png`

Purpose: gold/brass paperclip.

Aspect ratio: 1:2.  
Recommended size: 500 × 900 px.  
Colors: muted brass, warm highlight.

## `binder-ring-brass-01.png`

Purpose: brass binder ring or circular ring.

Aspect ratio: 1:1.  
Recommended size: 700 × 700 px.  
Colors: muted brass, darker inner edge.

Masking: center hole transparent.

## `thumbtack-cream-01.png`

Purpose: cream thumbtack for pinning paper pieces.

Aspect ratio: 1:1.  
Recommended size: 600 × 600 px.  
Colors: cream tack head, small gray pin stem, soft shadow.

## `pushpin-sage-01.png`

Purpose: sage pushpin.

Aspect ratio: 1:1.  
Recommended size: 600 × 600 px.  
Colors: sage head, gray pin, soft shadow.

## Acceptance checklist

- transparent outside object
- black matte removed
- usable at small sizes
- not too realistic or shiny
- object shadow does not create a rectangular halo
