# 2026-06-28 — Store proof/gallery calm budget

## Changed

- Refined `app/store-proof-gallery-terminal-budget-pass.css` instead of adding a new competing Store visual pass.
- Calmed the Store proof rail and slow shelf labels by reducing letter spacing, opacity, rotations, and repeated per-card transforms.
- Flattened Store preview/gallery card silhouettes back to cut-paper shapes with lighter shadows and no filters.
- Preserved the existing public-safe proof language: `preview proof`, `proof only`, `not inventory`, and `shelf proof path` remain visible.
- Kept mobile and reduced-motion behavior explicit so the proof/gallery shelf stays readable without unnecessary motion or heavy effects.

## Why

The current Store page had a late terminal budget for the proof rail and gallery shelf, but those two newest layers could still read as separate stacked decorative systems. The supported goal from `.agent` is not more accumulation; it is a calm, paper-first room where visual proof surfaces feel honest, organized, and performant. This pass keeps the existing Store preview content and no-fake-inventory boundary while making the proof rail and slow shelf scan as one quieter cut-paper sequence.
