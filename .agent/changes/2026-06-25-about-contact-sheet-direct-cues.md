# About contact sheet direct cue pass

Timestamp: 2026-06-25 20:30 ET

## Changed

- Replaced decorative numbered frame text in the About contact sheet with direct `Studio frame` paper tags and separate public orientation labels.
- Added a small tag row style to `app/about-contact-sheet-pass.css` so the cue reads as two clipped paper snippets instead of a numeric marker string.
- Updated the pages matrix to record the About contact sheet's non-numbered frame cue treatment.

## Why

The About contact sheet was already a paper/photo sequence, but each card still showed a decorative `01` / `02` style marker in the copy area. Active direct-label lessons prefer visible orientation and navigation surfaces without numbering or extra marker language. This pass keeps the contact sheet useful and papery while making the cue more human-facing and non-numeric.
