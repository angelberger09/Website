# Botanical Asset Specs

These assets soften the paper system and connect the site back to the Soft Strange Studio garden/cottage/craft language.

## Shared requirements

- Folder: `public/paper/botanicals/`
- Final format: transparent PNG
- Preview matte: black background
- Style: pressed botanical, light illustration/photo-collage hybrid, muted natural colors
- Use: card decorations, note clusters, source/reference cards, future about/gallery details

## `leaf-sprig-sage-01.png`

Purpose: primary sage leaf sprig.

Aspect ratio: 2:3 vertical.  
Recommended size: 700 × 1000 px.  
Colors: sage green, muted olive shadows, pale stem.

Masking: transparent around every leaf and stem.

Use: Notes card icon and small paper collages.

## `leaf-sprig-sage-02.png`

Purpose: alternate sage sprig with different silhouette.

Aspect ratio: 2:3 vertical.  
Recommended size: 700 × 1000 px.  
Content: thinner branch, more spaced leaves.

## `leaf-stem-pressed-01.png`

Purpose: pressed leaf stem.

Aspect ratio: 2:3 vertical.  
Recommended size: 700 × 1000 px.  
Colors: olive, muted brown stem, flattened pressed-paper texture.

Use: source/reference cards.

## `leaf-stem-pressed-02.png`

Purpose: alternate pressed stem.

Aspect ratio: 2:3 vertical.  
Recommended size: 700 × 1000 px.  
Content: paired leaves, slightly darker edge.

## `tiny-branch-olive-01.png`

Purpose: small olive branch accent.

Aspect ratio: 3:4 vertical.  
Recommended size: 650 × 860 px.  
Colors: olive green, muted yellow-green highlights.

## `pressed-flower-cream-01.png`

Purpose: cream pressed flower.

Aspect ratio: 1:1 or 3:4.  
Recommended size: 800 × 900 px.  
Colors: cream petals, ochre center, muted green stem.

## `pressed-flower-lavender-01.png`

Purpose: lavender pressed flower.

Aspect ratio: 1:1 or 3:4.  
Recommended size: 800 × 900 px.  
Colors: dusty lavender petals, muted green stem.

## `mushroom-sketch-mini-01.png`

Purpose: tiny mushroom sketch or cutout.

Aspect ratio: 1:1.  
Recommended size: 700 × 700 px.  
Style: soft graphite/ink sketch with cream cutout backing or transparent line art.

Use: optional Soft Strange accent and future art pages.

## Acceptance checklist

- transparent outside botanical shape
- edges are clean enough for dark and light backgrounds
- colors are muted and not neon
- decoration should not overpower text
- botanical style feels handmade/pressed, not stock clipart
