# Background Wash Asset Specs

These assets create the page atmosphere behind paper pieces.

## Shared requirements

- Folder: `public/paper/backgrounds/`
- Final format: transparent PNG when used as overlay; opaque PNG only if intentionally a full background
- Preview matte: black background is acceptable for edge checking, but final should layer softly over site cream
- Style: watercolor wash, soft pastel, no hard edges, no busy texture

## `wash-peach-right-01.png`

Purpose: warm peach wash on the right side of the page.

Aspect ratio: 16:9.  
Recommended size: 2400 × 1500 px.  
Colors: peach/coral `#ffb08f`, warm cream, pale apricot.  
Shape: soft irregular watercolor bloom fading to transparent.

Use: right-side hero background and warm page atmosphere.

## `wash-lavender-left-01.png`

Purpose: lavender wash on the left or lower-left side.

Aspect ratio: 16:9.  
Recommended size: 2400 × 1500 px.  
Colors: dusty lavender `#d8c5ec`, pale blue-gray, cream fade.

Use: balances peach warmth and creates depth behind title.

## `wash-cream-center-01.png`

Purpose: soft cream center light field.

Aspect ratio: 16:9.  
Recommended size: 2400 × 1500 px.  
Colors: warm cream, pale ivory, almost transparent center glow.

Use: improves text readability while keeping paper texture.

## `wash-sage-soft-01.png`

Purpose: soft sage wash.

Aspect ratio: 16:9.  
Recommended size: 2400 × 1500 px.  
Colors: sage `#b9c9af`, muted olive-gray, cream.

Use: behind cards or polaroid stacks.

## `wash-mixed-soft-01.png`

Purpose: combined peach/lavender/sage background wash.

Aspect ratio: 16:9.  
Recommended size: 2400 × 1500 px.  
Content: broad soft blend with no hard edges.

Use: full-page background option.

## `vignette-paper-wash-01.png`

Purpose: subtle vignette/fade to hold the center of a section.

Aspect ratio: 16:9.  
Recommended size: 2400 × 1500 px.  
Colors: transparent center, slightly darker warm paper edges.

Use: section framing without borders.

## Acceptance checklist

- washes should feel painted, not airbrushed plastic
- no black matte in final transparent exports
- low enough contrast that paper cards remain the focus
- edges fade naturally to transparency
