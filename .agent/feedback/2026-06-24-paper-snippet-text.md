# Paper snippet text feedback

Timestamp: 2026-06-24 22:58 ET

Status: active feedback

Source: Screenshot review of `angelberger09/Website` Store page.

## Public-safe summary

Text should feel like it lives on smaller paper snippets, labels, strips, or note pieces instead of sitting inside large generic UI panels.

## Extracted signal

Use smaller paper surfaces for text. Headings, labels, and supporting copy should feel clipped, pasted, pinned, or arranged as paper snippets in the collage system.

## Target state

- Text appears on paper snippets where appropriate.
- Copy blocks feel authored and placed.
- Large generic text containers are reduced.
- Hierarchy comes from paper placement, size, and grouping.

## Affected items

- PAGE-005 Store
- COMP-008 Filled Page Sections
- DESIGN-006 Human-Facing Copy
- DESIGN-022 Paper Piece Card Surfaces
- METRIC-021 Paper Cards Not UI Cards

## Implementation boundary

Use snippet surfaces for presentation without making copy noisy, tiny, or hard to scan.
