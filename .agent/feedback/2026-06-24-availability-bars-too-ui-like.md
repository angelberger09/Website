# Availability bars too UI-like feedback

Timestamp: 2026-06-24 22:58 ET

Status: active feedback

Source: Screenshot review of `angelberger09/Website` Store page availability states section.

## Public-safe summary

The horizontal availability rows still read like UI strips. They should feel more like collage strips, clipped paper labels, or physically placed paper snippets.

## Extracted signal

Repeated long bars should not dominate the section. The availability information can be broken into more tactile paper snippets or arranged pieces with stronger material edge logic.

## Target state

- Availability states feel like paper pieces, not app rows.
- The repeated horizontal-strip pattern is reduced or visually transformed.
- Paper edges, shadows, and placement carry the hierarchy.
- The information remains clear and scannable.
