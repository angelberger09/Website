## Interface mismatch

The Notes support strip was already paper-styled, but it could still read as three neighboring note cards between larger composed room boards. That left the calm-room and paper-collage goals only partially satisfied for this mid-page support area.

## Visible correction

`app/notes-support-room-docket-pass.css` turns `.notes-support-strip` into one visible reader-support docket with:

- a clipped shared paper backing field,
- a rendered docket label,
- a stitched thread running behind the notes,
- taped note-piece treatment,
- staggered desktop rhythm,
- single-column mobile fallback,
- reduced-motion safeguards.

## Quality metric

The section should be recognizable at a glance as one composed Notes-room support object, not a loose row of UI cards. It must keep labels and body copy readable and avoid adding private writing material, fake posts, or new data.
