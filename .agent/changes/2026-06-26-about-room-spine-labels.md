# 2026-06-26 About room spine labels

## Changed

- Added `aboutRoomSpine` data in `app/about/page.jsx` for the About page's major room sequence.
- Rendered a visible `about-room-spine` paper trail between the Room key and the main About boards.
- Added `app/about-section-spine-labels-pass.css` so the spine appears as five clipped paper slips connected by a soft thread, with a vertical mobile fallback.
- Imported the new pass from `app/layout.jsx`.
- Updated `.agent/matrices/pages.matrix.md` to record the new About room-path structure.

## Why

The active calm-room and flattened-hierarchy lessons were only partially satisfied on About. The page had strong individual boards, but the board sequence could still feel like separate paper sections rather than one composed public room path. A visible room spine gives visitors a lightweight map across Room key, Studio shape, Contact sheet, Principles, and Next doors without adding fake content or private material.

## Satisfaction state
