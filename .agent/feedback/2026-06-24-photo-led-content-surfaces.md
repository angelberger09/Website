# Photo-led content surface feedback

Timestamp: 2026-06-24 22:58 ET

Status: active feedback

Source: Screenshot review of `angelberger09/Website` Store page.

## Public-safe summary

Content should be carried more by photo-like or image-like surfaces, with text acting as snippets around the content, instead of relying mostly on text-heavy UI bars.

## Extracted signal

The page should shift toward a collage pattern where paper snippets hold text and photo/image surfaces carry content weight. Visual content should become structural, not only decorative.

## Target state

- More content-bearing photo or image surfaces.
- Text snippets support the visual content.
- Store/portfolio content feels like a scrapbook or editorial board.
- The layout avoids long repeated text bars when a visual card/photo surface would communicate better.
