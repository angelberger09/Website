# LESSON-014 — Make hero typography feel like art lettering

Timestamp: 2026-06-24 00:18 ET  
Context: Homepage type treatment feedback  
Status: active

## Lesson

The hero title should feel like soft art lettering: printed or drawn, playful, readable, and studio-made.

## Future rule

Treat the hero as printed/drawn studio lettering: softer display font stack, weight around 700–800, gentler letter spacing, more open line-height, softer ink color, and a paper-list composition.

## Applies to

- PAGE-001 Homepage
- COMP-003 Scroll Title Sequence
- DESIGN-002 Large Editorial Type
- DESIGN-013 Art Lettering Typography
- DESIGN-014 Paper List Hero Lockup

## Metric

The hero should feel handmade, playful, and readable rather than overly heavy or rigid.
