# Note Scrap Asset Specs

These assets create the sketchbook/studio desk language: notepads, tags, lined notes, grid notes, and memo scraps.

## Shared requirements

- Folder: `public/paper/notes/`
- Final format: transparent PNG
- Preview matte: black background
- Style: small handmade note scraps with transparent outside edges
- Paper colors: cream, aged beige, pale gray lines, warm graphite details
- Use: decorative icons inside cards, future blog headers, small collage clusters

## `spiral-notepad-small-01.png`

Purpose: small spiral notebook piece for the Notes card.

Aspect ratio: 4:3.  
Recommended size: 900 × 680 px.  
Content: cream lined notepad with visible spiral rings at top, faint horizontal ruled lines, slightly torn lower edge.

Colors: cream paper, warm gray lines, muted brass/graphite spiral.

Masking: transparent around notepad and between any ring holes if possible.

## Acceptance checklist

- all outer backgrounds transparent
- black preview matte fully removed
- lines/grid are low contrast and do not fight text
- edges feel paper-torn, not pixelated
- assets can be rotated/scaled in CSS without obvious artifacts
